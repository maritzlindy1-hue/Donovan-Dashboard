Management Focus Notes

Suggested talking points to keep the dashboard practical and action-driven.

  • Watch any ticket older than 6 days — these are immediate escalation or recovery items.
  • Use Days in Status to spot tickets that are stale even if they are already in the right process step.
  • Track tickets in client-feedback, quote, and install-waiting stages closely because they tend to stall.
  • Ensure every call, email, and follow-up is noted on the JC comments with contact person and outcome.
  • Use the Water/Electrical split to see where the workload concentration is and whether special support is needed.
